8. Appendices#

In this appendix, we provide essential statistical tables that are frequently used in hypothesis testing and other statistical analyses. These tables include:

  • Normal Distribution Table: This table provides the cumulative probability values for the standard normal distribution, which is crucial for calculating probabilities and critical values in various statistical tests.

  • t-Distribution Table: This table lists the critical values of the t-distribution, which is used when dealing with small sample sizes or when the population standard deviation is unknown.
