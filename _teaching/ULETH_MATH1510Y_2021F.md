<p style='text-align: justify;'>
This course covers various topics related to elementary functions, focusing on differentiation techniques such as the chain and product rules. It also explores extrema problems and integration. The course emphasizes the practical applications of these mathematical concepts in management, humanities, and the social sciences. Students will learn how to apply these mathematical tools to analyze real-world scenarios and make informed decisions in various fields.
</p>
