<p style='text-align: justify;'>
This course provides a comprehensive overview of the principles and topics defining the field of data science and analytics. The primary objective is to guide students through the entire data science lifecycle, from data acquisition and cleansing to exploration, modeling, visualization, and interpretation.

Through hands-on labs and exercises, students will utilize Generative AI, Jupyter Notebooks, and open-source analytical tools (including Python, R, and SQL) to apply coding skills in real-world scenarios. Additionally, the course incorporates critical discussions on the ethical implications of data science, ensuring students develop a keen awareness of bias and responsible analysis.
