<p style='text-align: justify;'>
This course immerses students in the realm of exploratory data analysis, providing them with a succinct introduction to probability theory and inferential statistics. Throughout the course, students will extensively explore essential topics, including making inferences on population means and proportions, conducting the chi-square test, and examining regression and correlation. Additionally, students will delve into the practical applications of these statistical methods, focusing particularly on their relevance within the field of medical and health sciences.
</p>
