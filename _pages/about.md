My industry experience includes roles as a Data Scientist and Research Scientist, where I developed predictive models, designed data pipelines, and implemented large-scale analytics using Python, SQL, and cloud computing platforms.
<br><br>
At the University of Missouri, I teach and develop graduate courses in natural language processing, geospatial AI, cloud computing, and time series analysis. As an AI Faculty Fellow, I contribute to advancing AI pedagogy, policy development, and faculty training. I also lead curriculum reviews, coordinate research seminars, and serve on graduate admissions committees.
<br><br>
I am committed to inclusive education and professional mentorship, actively engaging in thesis supervision, journal reviewing, and academic leadership. I founded the SIAM Chapter at the University of Calgary and continue to promote collaborative, interdisciplinary learning environments. Outside of academia, I enjoy hiking and spending time with family and friends, which continually inform my perspective as an educator and researcher.
