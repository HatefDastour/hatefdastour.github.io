Starbucks Offer Personalizations¶

In this article, simulated data that mimics customer behavior on the Starbucks rewards mobile app is used. Starbucks tends to send out offers to users of the mobile app once every few days. These offers are exclusive, that is not all users receive the same offer. An offer can contains a discount for their products or sometimes BOGO (buy one get one free). These offers have a validity period before the offer expires. The article here is inspired by a towardsdatascience.com article.
